About VT KASA

The purpose of the Korean American Student Association is to promote awareness and interest in Korean culture. Our goal is to make the Virginia Tech community a place where every student is respected and understood to allow this campus to be inclusive to all kinds of people with this culture. We promote Korean American unity in the campus, as well as address problems that Korean Americans face to Administrations here at Virginia Tech.
